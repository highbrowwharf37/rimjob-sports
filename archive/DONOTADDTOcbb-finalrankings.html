🥿 Cinderella Watch

Teams most likely to make a deep tournament run based on efficiency, tempo, defensive strength, and seed vs. true ability gap

How we identify Cinderella teams:
Research shows the best statistical predictors of tournament upsets are: Adjusted Efficiency Margin (teams outperforming their seed), Defensive Efficiency (elite defenses disrupt favored opponents), Barthag vs Seed Gap (teams whose true strength far exceeds their projected seed), Tempo Control (teams that play at their own pace), and Schedule-adjusted WAB (wins above bubble vs difficulty of schedule). The Cinderella Score below is a composite of all five factors.
